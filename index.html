Subscription FAQ

How do subscriptions work?
Choose Pro or Business, complete checkout via Stripe, and your account unlocks automatically when you sign in with the same email. Subscriptions renew each billing period until you cancel.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your Stripe receipt or customer portal — you keep access through the end of your billing period.
Subscription vs credits?
Subscriptions give unlimited generations. Credits are one-time packs for occasional use — no recurring charge, and they never expire.
What if I paid before creating an account?
Sign up with the same email you used at checkout. Your plan or credits will apply automatically.
How does API access work?
Business subscribers open Account to generate API keys. Full reference at /api-docs — authentication, streaming, examples in cURL, Node, and Python.

Buy credits instead

No subscription. Pay as you go. Credits never expire. Most templates cost 1 credit; longer documents cost 2–3.
